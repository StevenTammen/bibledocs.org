BibleDocs runs two online communities: 

1. The [BibleDocs Ichthys Community](/meta/bibledocs-online-communities/bibledocs-ichthys-community) is a *private* (i.e., invite-only) online community designed to give followers of [Ichthys.com](https://ichthys.com/) and related ministries a place to connect with and interact with one another. The [BibleDocs Ichthys Bible Study](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies/bibledocs-ichthys-bible-study)---one of the two [BibleDocs Weekly Bible Studies](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies) that BibleDocs runs---is associated with this community. For details on how to join the BibleDocs Ichthys Community group chats, [see here](/meta/bibledocs-online-communities/bibledocs-ichthys-community/#joining-the-bibledocs-ichthys-community-group-chats).
2. The [BibleDocs Open Community](/meta/bibledocs-online-communities/bibledocs-open-community) is a *public* (i.e., anyone can join) online community designed to give people comfortable with making certain presuppositions about inspiration, inerrancy, and *sola scriptura* a place to discuss exegesis and interpretation in depth. The [BibleDocs Open Bible Study](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies/bibledocs-open-bible-study)---one of the two [BibleDocs Weekly Bible Studies](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies) that BibleDocs runs---is associated with this community. For details on how to join the BibleDocs Open Community group chats, [see here](/meta/bibledocs-online-communities/bibledocs-open-community/#joining-the-bibledocs-open-community-group-chats).


For details on [meeting dates and times](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies/#meeting-dates-and-times), [joining us in person](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies/#joining-us-in-person), and [joining us over Zoom](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies/#joining-us-over-zoom), see the [overview page for BibleDocs Weekly Bible Studies](/meta/bibledocs-weekly-bible-studies).

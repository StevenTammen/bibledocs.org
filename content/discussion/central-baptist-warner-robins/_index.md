This section contains discussion series based on studies from Central Baptist - Warner Robins.

Central Baptist is a physical church located in Warner Robins, GA. It is relatively in line with the doctrine of other similar churches in the Southern Baptist Convention. The ["What we believe" page](https://www.centralwr.com/what-we-believe) for Central Baptist reproduces *The Baptist Faith and Message* from the SBC, for example.

I would be classified by most as an independent Evangelical, and do not formally identify with any denomination, including the Baptists. There are parts of conventional Baptist doctrine that I do not agree with, and that goes for Central too since it is not particularly outside the norm as far as large Baptist churches go (as far as I can see). I am just saying this by way of explanation, inasmuch as what I teach may not be the same as what this church's official teachings are.

Some or all of the series we've done in Sunday school have come from [Lifeway](https://www.lifeway.com/en/about). According to Lifeway:

BibleDocs

Scripture-centered writings for the Christian life

Overview Resources Contact

BibleDocs is a site dedicated to in-depth Bible teaching and resources related to Bible study, apologetics, evangelism, and more.

All Christians should read their Bible often, no matter what sort of role they occupy in the body of Christ. However, God has provided gifted individuals in the Church to supply Christians with additional spiritual nourishment (Ephesians 4:11-16). Pastor-teachers, for example, exercise their spiritual gifts to mine truth from scripture and present it in different forms to others, according to the ministries assigned to them by Christ (1 Corinthians 12:4-6). Bible teaching does not replace Bible reading; rather, it supplements it. Likewise, apologetics and evangelism helps never supplant the Bible, but are used alongside it to make ministry more effective.

The materials on this site are written with this perspective in mind: that scripture is the final authority in all things, and that Bible teaching, apologetics, evangelism, etc. must necessarily stem directly from the Bible, being undertaken by gifted and prepared individuals.

Bible-centered

All materials on this site strive to keep the focus on the Biblical text. Greek and Hebrew are brought to bear as necessary, along with, e.g., hermeneutics and textual criticism. Conjecture and application are clearly distinguished from teaching.

Application-aware

Christians are called to learn Biblical truth, believe it, apply it in the real world under shot and shell, and then help others do likewise. While the specifics are always personal, this site structures content to help make application straightforward.

Level-appropriate

Different Christians are at different places in their spiritual walk. Some are ready for advanced materials, while others are not quite ready yet. To make content useful to everyone, all writing on this site comes in three levels of technical detail.

BibleDocs has different sorts of resources suitable for different things.

At a high level, all Christians need to grow to spiritual maturity, pass tests of their faith until it is "battle hardened," and then come into their own ministries. This site puts an emphasis on materials that directly contribute to this progression. However, specialized materials are appropriate as well: those going into evangelism should be familiar with some of the common stumbling blocks in faith unbelievers face (such as blaming God for tragic events), those going into apologetics are going to need to know something of science and philosophy, those going into some form of Christian counseling and encouragement would do well to study how humans process emotions, and so on.

Types of resources offered

This site offers 6 categories of resources:

  1. Topical studies
  2. In-depth verse-by-verse studies
  3. More abbreviated, less interpretive notes on scripture, in the manner of a study Bible
  4. Guides on various matters (e.g., using Bible study software)
  5. Preparation and ministry information for specific callings (e.g., apologetics, evangelism, music)
  6. Questions and answers by topic (some questions come from correspondence with site readers, others are of my own creation; all answers are mine)

These 6 categories of resources are organized independently, with content available via equivalent webpages, PDFs, and eBooks. Since text is irreplaceable as a study medium, it is the dominant content type for this site.

Have questions?

A central part of this ministry is fielding and answering questions from everyday Christians (and then topically organizing and posting the questions and their respective answers once enough of them have accumulated), so please do not be shy to reach out. You'll be helping the site in the long run!
